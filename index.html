Calm Steps

A gentle guide to finding your center.

01. Breathe

Inhale for 4 seconds... Hold for 4... Exhale for 8.

02. Grounding

Look around you. Name 5 things you can see, 4 you can touch, and 3 you can hear.

03. Acceptance

"This feeling is uncomfortable, but I am safe. It will pass like a cloud in the sky."
