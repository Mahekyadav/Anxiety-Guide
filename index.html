Immediate Help & Resources

If you're in crisis or having thoughts of self-harm, please reach out now. You are not alone.

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

988 or 1-800-273-8255

Available 24/7 for free, confidential support

Call Now

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741

Free, 24/7 crisis counseling via text message

Additional Resources

Trevor Project (LGBTQ+): 1-866-488-7386

Veterans Crisis Line: 1-800-273-8255, press 1

Disaster Distress Helpline: 1-800-985-5990

Create a Safety Plan

If you're feeling unsafe, consider these steps:

  1. Remove means of self-harm from your environment
  2. Contact a trusted friend or family member
  3. Go to a public place where you feel safe
  4. Call one of the helplines above
  5. Visit the nearest emergency room

Your life matters. Please reach out for help. You are worthy of support and care.
